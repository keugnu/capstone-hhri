Node:	near_or_far (Spoken declaration of whether or not something is nearby)
Input:
	Data (float) from the sonar sensor (representing meters).
Config: 
	Distance (float): 0.5 m - "Near," otherwise far
Output:
	"Near" or "far" audio.
Steps:
	1. Initialization: control distance is 0.5 meters.
	2. if distance is greater than or equal to 0.5 then:
		(a) Send "Far" audio to speaker.
	3. else if distance is less than 0.5 then:
		(a) Send "Near" audio to speaker.
