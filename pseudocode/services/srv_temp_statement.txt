
Node:	temperature_statement (Spoken declaration of whether or not something is hot)
Input:
	Data (float) from temperature sensor (degrees Fahrenheit).
Config: 
	Temperature (float): 85.0 degrees (F) - "hot," otherwise "not hot."
Output:
	"Hot" or "Not hot" audio.
Steps:
	1. Initialization: control temperature is 85.0 degrees.
	2. if temperature is greater than or equal to 85.0 then:
		(a) Send "Hot" audio to speaker.
	3. else if temperature is less than 85.0 then:
		(a) Send "Not hot" audio to speaker.
