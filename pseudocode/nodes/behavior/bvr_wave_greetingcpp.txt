
Node:	wave_greeting (Speech response to waving gesture)
Input:
	Data from the gesture sensor.
Config: 
	Gesture: hand moving left-to-right and immediately right-to-left over gesture sensor.
Output:
	Greeting audio (String).
Steps:
	1. Initialization: begin subscribing to tpc_gesture (5Hz)
	2. Recognize if input from gesture sensor matches config.
	3. Send greeting audio to speaker.
